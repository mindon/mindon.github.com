Finally My Nexus 7 updated to 4.4 KitKat

This morning, the OTA Android KitKat 4.4 finally reached my Nexus 7 (2012) and Aby’s Nexus 7 (2013). It’s a long time waiting.

Nov. 13, 2013 Google announced that its Nexus 7 and Nexus 10 tablets are getting Android 4.4 KitKat. After clicking check update 13 days later, it’s such a long time :-p

There’s no ART in KitKat for Nexus 7 (2012). ( ART runtime is missing in Android 4.4 on Nexus 7 (2012) )
