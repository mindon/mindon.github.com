today, at my full 28,

everything is the same,

just like every single day,

still grey…

what’s next i could make,

with nothing i could say,

but wait…

something great,

something fade,

now save…

i should not wave,

without the open gate,

ever evade…

i wait,

in my way,

but it’s always

gone without any trace,

being away…

no name…

no face…

no mate…

no fate…

i pay,

i gain,

i take,

for safe,

to stay.

hey,

it’s my birthday,

a single day.
