Print the Schengen VISA Application Form in A3

We are preparing the documents for the Schengen VISA to Italy & France.

It’s such a big project for a 15 days journey, and it will be our first time to Europe – attracted by its cultrue, landscape & fresh air :p

Here the tips to print the Italy VISA application form in a A3 paper. Yes, I mean the printer settings(打印机设置):

First,

  • Page Size（页面尺寸）: A4

  • Output Size（输出尺寸）: A3

Second,

  • Layout（布局）: 2 in 1

Third,

  • Double Side（双面打印）

  • Short Edge （短边装订）

Last!

  • Print pages（打印页面）: 4,1,2,3

The point is we could specify the order of pages.
